## Overview

This framework documents proven patterns for building and scaling an enterprise B2B technology partner program. All specific company names and identifying details have been removed - only the processes, structures, and playbooks remain.

---

## Program Architecture

### Three-Tier Partner Model

**Bronze (Registered)**
- Self-service enablement portal access
- Basic sales materials
- Deal registration protection
- Annual Revenue Target: <$100K

**Silver (Certified)**
- Technical certification requirements
- Co-marketing access
- Priority lead distribution
- Dedicated partner manager
- Annual Revenue Target: $100K-$500K

**Gold (Strategic)**
- Executive sponsor assignments
- Custom enablement tracks
- Joint GTM planning
- Co-selling motions
- Quarterly business reviews
- Annual Revenue Target: $500K+

| P2 - High | Major feature broken, many affected | 1 hour | 24 hours |
