# Partner Commission Structure

## OVERVIEW

This commission structure applies to all authorized partners under the PartnerOS Partner Program. Commission rates are determined by partner tier and deal type.

## COMMISSION TIERS

### Base Commission Rates
| Partner Tier | Commission Rate | Annual Revenue Target |
|--------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Bronze | [15]% | $0 - $99,999 |
| Silver | [20]% | $100,000 - $499,999 |
| Gold | [25]% | $500,000+ |

### Tier Qualification Rules
- Tier assessed [quarterly / annually]
- Must meet [revenue AND certification] requirements
- Tier valid for [12] months from qualification

## COMMISSION TYPES

### 1. Partner-Sourced Revenue
Revenue from deals where partner is the primary influence:

| Tier | Commission Rate |
|------|----------------|
| Bronze | 15% |
| Silver | 20% |
| Gold | 25% |

**Eligibility:**
- Partner must be registered as source in deal registration system
- Deal must close within [90] days of registration

### 2. Partner-Influenced Revenue
Revenue from deals where partner assisted but wasn't primary:

| Tier | Commission Rate |
|------|----------------|
| Bronze | 7.5% |
| Silver | 10% |
| Gold | 12.5% |

### 3. Recurring Revenue (SaaS)
For subscription-based products:

| Year | Commission Rate |
|------|----------------|
| Year 1 | 20% |
| Year 2+ | 10% |

### 4. Professional Services
For implementation and consulting services:

| Service Type | Commission Rate |
|--------------|----------------|
| Standard Implementation | 10% |
| Custom Development | 15% |

## BONUS INCENTIVES

### Accelerators
| Revenue Threshold | Accelerator |
|-------------------|-------------|
| 110% of quota | +5% bonus |
| 125% of quota | +10% bonus |
| 150% of quota | +15% bonus |

### New Partner Bonus
- First deal: [+10%] bonus commission
- First [3] deals: [+5%] bonus per deal

### Certification Bonus
- [2] certified reps: +2.5% commission
- [4] certified reps: +5% commission

## DEAL REGISTRATION

### Registration Requirements
- Prospect must not exist in Company CRM
- Opportunity value > $[minimum]
- Expected close date within [90] days

### Protection Period
- Exclusive: [60] days
- Non-exclusive: [30] days

### Registration Rewards
| Scenario | Commission |
|----------|------------|
| Registered + Closed by Partner | Standard rate |
| Registered + Partner-assisted | Influenced rate |
| Registered + Closed by Company | 50% of standard |

## PAYMENT TERMS

### Payment Schedule
- Commissions paid [monthly / quarterly]
- Payment within [30] days of month-end
- Minimum payout: $[100]

### Payment Methods
- [ ] Direct deposit
- [ ] Check
- [ ] Wire transfer

### Payment Documentation
- Commission statement emailed by [X] of each month
- Detailed breakdown by deal
- Adjustments from prior periods noted

## CLARIFICATIONS

### What Counts Toward Revenue
- [ ] New logo deals
- [ ] Existing customer expansions
- [ ] Renewals (at standard rate)
- [ ] Professional services

### What Does NOT Count
- Internal/company deals
- Competitor replacements (unless approved)
- Deals below minimum threshold

### Clawback Provisions
- Cancelled/refunded deals: Commission reclaimed
- Customer non-payment: Commission reclaimed
- Deal reversed within [90] days: Commission adjusted

## EXAMPLE CALCULATIONS

### Example 1: Bronze Partner
```
Deal: $50,000 new SaaS subscription
Type: Partner-sourced
Commission: 50,000 × 15% = $7,500
```

### Example 2: Gold Partner with Accelerator
```
Deal: $200,000 new SaaS subscription
Type: Partner-sourced
Annual Revenue: $600,000 (Gold tier)
Base Commission: 200,000 × 25% = $50,000
Accelerator (+5% for 110%+ quota): 200,000 × 5% = $10,000
Total Commission: $60,000
```

### Example 3: Influenced Deal
```
Deal: $100,000 renewal
Type: Partner-influenced
Tier: Silver
Commission: 100,000 × 10% = $10,000
```
