## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
This model defines revenue sharing for strategic partnerships.

**Steps:**

1. **Define contribution** — What does each side bring? (1 hour)
2. **Set split** — Fair revenue allocation (1 hour)
3. **Document** — Create agreement (1 hour)

# Revenue Sharing Model

## Partnership Types

| Type | Description | Typical Split |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Co-sell** | Joint selling | 70/30 |
| **OEM** | Bundled product | 50/50 |
| **Referral** | Lead generation | 90/10 |

## Split Calculation

| Factor | Weight | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Lead generation | 30% | Who found the customer? |
| Sales effort | 40% | Who closed the deal? |
| Delivery | 30% | Who implements? |
