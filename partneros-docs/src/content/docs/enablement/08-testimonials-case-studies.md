> **Partner Testimonials & Case Study Guide**
> 
> A practical guide to collecting and publishing partner success stories.


## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
Transform successful partner relationships into compelling social proof that accelerates your sales cycle.

**Steps:**
1. Identify high-potential partners for testimonials
2. Craft interview questions that reveal success stories
3. Capture metrics and quantify impact
4. Create multiple asset formats (quote, video, full case study)
5. Get legal approval and publish

**Why It Works:**
- 92% of buyers trust peer recommendations over advertising
- Case studies convert 70% better than product descriptions
- Partner testimonials build credibility in your partner ecosystem

# Partner Testimonials & Case Study Guide

## 1. Identifying Testimonial Candidates

### Ideal Partner Profile

Look for partners who meet these criteria:

| Criteria | Why It Matters |
|----------|----------------|
| 6+ months active | Demonstrates sustained success |
| Strong relationship | More likely to participate |
| Measurable results | Quantifiable impact is compelling |
| Willingness to engage | Enthusiasm shows in the testimonial |
| Brand alignment | Their audience matters to you |

### Partner Tier Prioritization

**Gold Partners:**
- Full case study with executive interview
- Video testimonial
- Conference presentation
- Joint press release

**Silver Partners:**
- Written case study
- Quote + headshot
- Webinar appearance

**Bronze Partners:**
- Short testimonial quote
- Social media endorsement


---

## 2. Interview Questions That Reveal Stories

### The Success Story Framework

Ask these questions to uncover compelling narratives:

**The Beginning:**
- "What challenge were you facing before we started working together?"
- "What was the cost of that problem to your business?"

**The Decision:**
- "What made you decide to become a partner?"
- "What alternatives did you consider?"

**The Journey:**
- "What was the first win you experienced?"
- "What was unexpected or surprising?"
- "What was the hardest part of the journey?"

**The Results:**
- "What specific metrics have improved?"
- "Can you put a number on the impact?"
- "What's the ROI you've seen?"

**The Recommendation:**
- "Who would you recommend this program to?"
- "What would you tell someone considering partnership?"


---

## 3. Quantifying Impact

### Metrics That Sell

Collect these data points for maximum impact:

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Additional revenue generated
- Deal size increase
- Customer acquisition rate
- Repeat purchase rate

**Efficiency Metrics:**
- Time saved
- Cost reduction
- Process improvements
- Automation benefits

**Growth Metrics:**
- Partner revenue growth
- Market expansion
- Customer retention
- New capabilities gained

### The "Before & After" Format

```
BEFORE Partnership:
- Revenue from this channel: $0
- Time to close deals: 90 days
- Customer satisfaction: 3.2/5

AFTER Partnership:
- Revenue from this channel: $150K/year
- Time to close deals: 45 days
- Customer satisfaction: 4.8/5
```

## 4. Creating Multiple Asset Formats

### Quote Card (150 words)

**Format:** 800x800px image with quote + photo + logo

```
"[Key quote about the partnership outcome]"
— [Name], [Title], [Company]

[Your Company Logo]    [Partner Company Logo]
```

### Short Testimonial (300 words)

Structure:
1. The partner's challenge (2 sentences)
2. The solution/action taken (2 sentences)
3. The measurable result (2 sentences)
4. Recommendation (1 sentence)

### Full Case Study (1,500-2,000 words)

**Sections:**
1. Executive Summary (100 words)
2. The Challenge (200 words)
3. The Solution (300 words)
4. The Results (400 words) ← Focus most here
5. Looking Forward (100 words)
6. Partner Quote (50 words)

### Video Testimonial Script

**Duration:** 60-90 seconds

| Time | Content |
|------|---------|
| 0-10s | Who you are, company + role |
| 10-30s | What problem you had |
| 30-60s | What working with us was like |
| 60-90s | The results, in one sentence |

## 5. Legal & Approval Process

### Release Form Checklist

Before publishing, ensure you have:

- [ ] Written approval from partner executive
- [ ] Approved quote wording
- [ ] Photo/headshot release
- [ ] Logo usage permission
- [ ] Case study review and sign-off
- [ ] Legal team approval (if required)

### Sample Permission Request

```
Subject: Permission to Share Our Success Story

Hi [Partner Name],

We'd love to feature our partnership in a case study on our website. 
Is that something you'd be open to?

We're thinking it would include:
- A 2-minute video interview
- A written case study (~1,500 words)
- A quote for our homepage

You'll have full approval of the final content before we publish.
We can also include a reciprocal testimonial for your site.

Let me know if you're interested!

Best,
[Your Name]
```
