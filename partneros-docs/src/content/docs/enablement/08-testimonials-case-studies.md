# Partner Testimonials & Case Studies

## Testimonial Types

| Type | Length | Use Case |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Quote** | 1-2 sentences | Website, sales decks |
| **Video** | 1-3 minutes | Events, demos |
| **Written** | 200-500 words | Case studies, PDFs |

---

## Case Study Framework

### 1. The Challenge
- What problem did the customer face?
- Why did they need a solution?

### 2. The Solution
- What did the partner implement?
- Why did they choose this approach?

### 3. The Results
- Quantifiable outcomes
- Key metrics improved

### 4. The Testimonial
- Customer quote
- Contact for reference
