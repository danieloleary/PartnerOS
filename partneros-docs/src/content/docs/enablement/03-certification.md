# Partner Certification Program

## Certification Levels

| Level | Focus | Requirements | Benefits |
|-------|-------|--------------|----------|
| **Foundation** | Product basics | Online exam | Deal registration |
| **Advanced** | Sales & delivery | Exam + practical | Co-marketing |
| **Expert** | Complex deals | Exam + case study | Priority support |

---

## Exam Structure

### Foundation Exam
- 30 multiple choice questions
- 60 minutes
- 70% passing score
- Online proctored

### Advanced Exam
- 50 questions + 2 case studies
- 90 minutes
- 80% passing score
- Live proctoring
