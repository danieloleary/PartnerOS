## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
This scorecard assesses overall partner health.

**Steps:**

1. **Score** — Rate each dimension (30 min)
2. **Analyze** — Identify issues (30 min)
3. **Plan** — Create improvement plan (30 min)

# Partner Health Scorecard

## Health Dimensions

| Dimension | Weight | Score (1-10) |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| Revenue | 30% | /10 |
| Engagement | 20% | /10 |
| Capability | 20% | /10 |
| Growth | 15% | /10 |
| Satisfaction | 15% | /10 |
| **Total** | **100%** | **/10** |

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Health | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 8-10 | Healthy | Maintain |
| 5-7 | At Risk | Improve |
| <5 | Critical | Intervention |
