Data is the language of executives. This template helps you build dashboards that tell the partner program story.

:::tip[Insider Tip]
The best dashboards answer one question: "Are we winning?" If your CFO needs more than 30 seconds to understand your dashboard, it's too complex.
:::

## How to Use This Template

Design your analytics approach during program design. Build dashboards in Q1, refine quarterly.

## What I Wish I Knew

The biggest mistake? Building dashboards no one looks at. Start with 5 metrics, expand as needed.

The second mistake? Not having data ownership. Someone needs to own data quality.

The third mistake? Ignoring partner-facing dashboards. Partners want to see their performance too.
