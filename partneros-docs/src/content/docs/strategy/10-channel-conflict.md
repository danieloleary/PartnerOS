Channel conflict isn't if—it's when. Every successful partner program has faced it. This playbook ensures you survive it.

:::tip[Insider Tip]
The best channel conflict is prevented, not resolved. Deal registration, clear territories, and transparent rules prevent 90% of conflicts.
:::

## How to Use This Template

Create during program launch and review quarterly. Train all sales and partner teams.

---

## Types of Channel Conflict

### Type 1: Pricing Conflict

| Scenario | Example |
|----------|---------|
| Partner discounts | Partner offers better price |
| Special pricing | Direct gets better deal |

### Type 2: Territory Conflict

| Scenario | Example |
|----------|---------|
| Geographic overlap | Both selling in same region |
| Account conflict | Both targeting same logo |

### Type 3: Product Conflict

| Scenario | Example |
|----------|---------|
| Reseller vs direct | Partner sells direct |
| Competitor promotion | Partner promotes competitor |

## Prevention Rules

### Deal Registration

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Protection period | 90 days |
| Approval required | For extensions |
| Pricing rules | Must maintain margin |

### Territory Rules

| Rule | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Primary region | Partner's main focus |
| Open territory | Anyone can sell |
| Named accounts | Assigned to either channel |

### Pricing Rules

| Scenario | Decision |
|----------|----------|
| Partner quotes first | Partner wins |
| Direct quotes first | Direct wins |
| Same day | Both can proceed |

## Resolution Process

### Step 1: Identify (24 hours)

- Log in CRM with "conflict" tag
- Notify both channel managers

### Step 2: Investigate (48 hours)

- Review deal registration timestamp
- Check territory assignments
- Validate customer contact history

### Step 3: Resolve (72 hours)

| Scenario | Resolution |
|----------|------------|
| Partner registered first | Partner wins |
| Direct has existing relationship | Direct wins |
| Both registered same day | Flip coin or split |

### Step 4: Escalate (if needed)

| Level | Who | Timeline |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Channel Manager | Partner + Sales managers | 72 hours |
| VP Channel | VPs of both | 1 week |
| CEO | Both CEOs | 2 weeks |

### What Sales Needs to Know

| Question | Answer |

| Do partners compete with us? | No, they extend us |
| What if a partner steals my deal? | Deal registration protects you |
| Do I get credit for partner deals? | Yes, influence credit |

## What I Wish I Knew

The biggest mistake? Not having clear rules. Ambiguity breeds conflict.

The second mistake? Favoring one channel. Even perception of bias destroys trust.

The third mistake? Not involving sales early. If sales isn't aligned, the program fails.
