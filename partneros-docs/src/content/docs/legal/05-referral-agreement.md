Referral partners are the easiest way to start building an ecosystem. This agreement formalizes the relationship.

:::tip[Insider Tip]
Referral agreements are simpler than reseller agreements but still need legal review. Keep it to 3-5 pages maximum.
:::

## How to Use This Template

Use for referral-only partners who won't be reselling directly.

---

## Agreement Structure

### Part 1: Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Referral | A lead introduced to Company by Partner |
| Qualified Referral | A referral that becomes a customer |
| Referral Fee | Commission paid to Partner |
| Term | Agreement duration |

---

## Part 2: Obligations

### Partner Obligations

- [ ] Introduce qualified leads
- [ ] Provide company information
- [ ] Not represent as reseller
- [ ] Follow brand guidelines

### Company Obligations

- [ ] Pay referral fees on time
- [ ] Provide sales support
- [ ] Not compete with Partner's business

---

## Part 3: Referral Fees

### Fee Structure

| Customer Type | Referral Fee |
|--------------|--------------|
| New customer | 10% of first-year revenue |
| Existing customer expansion | 5% of expansion |
| Multi-year deal | 15% of total contract |

### Payment Terms

- Net 30 days after customer payment
- Minimum referral fee: $500

---

## Part 4: Exclusivity

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Non-exclusive | Either party can work with others |
| Exclusive (territory) | Company won't refer in Partner's territory |
| Exclusive (vertical) | Company won't refer in Partner's vertical |

---

## Part 5: Term & Termination

| Item | Terms |
|------|-------|
| Initial term | 1 year |
| Renewal | Automatic annual |
| Termination notice | 30 days |
| Post-termination | Fees for referrals made during term |
