# First Partner Onboarding Path

*The exact 4-week sequence to your first signed partner*

---

## Overview

This path guides you from "no partners" to "first partner signed and onboarded" in 4 weeks.

| Week | Focus | Templates Used |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Week 1 | Define & Find | IPP, Email Sequence |
| Week 2 | Qualify & Pitch | Qualification, Discovery, Pitch |
| Week 3 | Propose & Sign | Proposal, Agreement |
| Week 4 | Onboard | Onboarding Checklist, Roadmap |

---

## Week 1: Define & Find

*Goal: Know who you want and find them*

### Day 1-2: Define Your Ideal Partner

**Template:** [Ideal Partner Profile](../strategy/02-ideal-partner-profile.md/)

This defines:
- Company size and type
- Target market/industry
- Technical capabilities
- Revenue potential

**Output:** 1-page ideal partner description

### Day 3-4: Build Your Outreach List

**Template:** [ICP Alignment Tracker](../recruitment/10-icp-tracker.md/)

Document:
- 10-20 potential partners
- Contact names
- Initial qualification notes

### Day 5: Create Outreach Templates

**Template:** [Email Sequence](../recruitment/01-email-sequence.md/)

Create:
- Initial outreach email
- Follow-up email
- Value proposition email

---

## Week 2: Qualify & Pitch

*Goal: Have conversations with qualified prospects*

### Day 1-2: Qualify Prospects

**Template:** [Qualification Framework](../recruitment/03-qualification-framework.md/)

Score each prospect on:
- Fit score (1-10)
- Interest level
- Timeline
- Budget authority

### Day 3: Prepare for Discovery Call

**Template:** [Discovery Call Script](../recruitment/04-discovery-call.md/)

Customize:
- Questions to ask
- Information to gather
- Next steps

### Day 4-5: Conduct Discovery Calls

**Action:** Run calls with top 3-5 prospects

### Day 5: Prepare Custom Pitch

**Template:** [Partner Pitch Deck](../recruitment/05-pitch-deck.md/)

Customize for top prospect:
- Their pain points
- Your solution
- Partnership benefits

---

## Week 3: Propose & Sign

*Goal: Get verbal commitment and agreement*

### Day 1-2: Create Proposal

**Template:** [Proposal Template](../recruitment/07-proposal.md/)

Include:
- Partnership terms
- Revenue share/commission
- Support commitments
- Timeline

### Day 3: Send Proposal

**Action:** Send proposal, schedule follow-up

### Day 4-5: Negotiate & Verbal Agreement

**Action:**
- Address questions
- Negotiate terms
- Get verbal agreement

---

## Week 4: Onboard

*Goal: Get partner productive*

### Day 1: Sign Agreement

**Template:** [Agreement Template](../recruitment/08-agreement.md/)

Finalize:
- Full MSA or partnership agreement
- Signatures

### Day 2-3: Onboarding Checklist

**Template:** [Onboarding Checklist](../recruitment/09-onboarding.md/)

Complete:
- Portal access
- Initial training
- First call scheduled

### Day 4-5: Enable Partner

**Template:** [Enablement Roadmap](../enablement/01-roadmap.md/)

Create:
- 30-60-90 day plan
- Training schedule
- First targets

---

## Checklist Summary

### Week 1: Define & Find
- [ ] Ideal Partner Profile completed
- [ ] Prospect list of 10-20 companies
- [ ] Email templates created

### Week 2: Qualify & Pitch
- [ ] All prospects qualified (score 7+)
- [ ] Discovery calls completed (3-5)
- [ ] Custom pitch deck prepared

### Week 3: Propose & Sign
- [ ] Proposal sent to top prospect
- [ ] Verbal agreement obtained
- [ ] Agreement drafted

### Week 4: Onboard
- [ ] Agreement signed
- [ ] Onboarding checklist started
- [ ] Enablement roadmap created

## Common Questions

### What if I don't have a prospect list?
Start with your existing customers who could become partners, or companies you admire in your space.

### What if prospects say no?
Use the [Email Sequence](../recruitment/01-email-sequence.md/) follow-ups. Keep building relationships.

### How long should each week take?
This is a guide - adjust based on your timeline. Some companies do this in 2 weeks, others in 8.
