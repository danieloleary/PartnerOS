> **The first 30 days define the partnership. Get them productive fast or watch momentum die.** — Dan O'Leary

:::tip[Insider Tip]
Partners who complete onboarding in 30 days are 3x more likely to generate revenue in Year 1. Speed matters.
:::

## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
This checklist ensures new partners are set up for success quickly. It provides a systematic approach to onboarding.

**Why It Works:**
Partners who aren't onboarded properly underperform. This checklist ensures nothing falls through the cracks.

**Steps:**

1. **Week 1** — Setup and orientation
2. **Week 2** — Training and certification
3. **Week 3** — Enablement and first deals
4. **Week 4** — Review and optimization

---

# Partner Onboarding Checklist

## Week 1: Setup & Orientation

### Administrative
- [ ] Send welcome email with next steps
- [ ] Execute partnership agreement
- [ ] Set up partner portal access
- [ ] Create CRM account
- [ ] Assign partner manager
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call

### Kickoff Meeting Agenda
1. Introduction to team
2. Partnership overview
3. Goals for first 90 days
4. Communication cadence
5. Resources overview

---

## Week 2: Training & Certification

### Product Training
- [ ] Product overview session
- [ ] Technical deep-dive
- [ ] Demo training
- [ ] Certification kickoff

### Sales Training
- [ ] Sales process overview
- [ ] Pitch deck review
- [ ] Objection handling
- [ ] Tools training

---

## Week 3: Enablement & First Deals

### Enablement
- [ ] Provide sales collateral
- [ ] Share battle cards
- [ ] Review success stories
- [ ] Co-marketing planning

### First Deal Support
- [ ] Identify first opportunity
- [ ] Joint call support
- [ ] Deal registration process
- [ ] Pricing and discounts

---

## Week 4: Review & Optimization

### 30-Day Checkpoint
- [ ] Review progress
- [ ] Address challenges
- [ ] Adjust plan as needed
- [ ] Set 60-day goals
- [ ] Schedule ongoing cadence

---
