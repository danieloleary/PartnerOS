> **Your pitch deck should answer one question: "Why should I spend my limited time and resources on this partnership instead of the other 10 things competing for my attention?"** — Dan O'Leary

:::tip INSIDER TIP
The best pitch decks are 10 slides maximum. If you need more, you don't have a clear enough message. Executives have 5 minutes—make every slide count.
:::

## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
This deck provides a compelling narrative for partner recruitment conversations. It's designed to convert interest into partnership decisions.

**Why It Works:**
A clear, consistent pitch builds credibility. When every partner conversation uses the same compelling narrative, conversion rates improve.

**Steps:**

1. **Customize slides** — Fill in company-specific content (1 hour)
2. **Add proof points** — Insert real success stories (30 min)
3. **Practice delivery** — Rehearse until natural (30 min)
4. **Iterate** — Update quarterly based on feedback (ongoing)

# Partner Pitch Deck

## The 10-Slide Framework

| Slide | Purpose | Time |
|-------|---------|------|
| 1. Title | Set the stage | 30 sec |
| 2. The Problem | Create urgency | 1 min |
| 3. Our Solution | Show we solve it | 1 min |
| 4. Market Opportunity | Prove the size | 1 min |
| 5. The Partnership | What we're offering | 2 min |
| 6. Success Stories | Prove it works | 2 min |
| 7. Partnership Economics | Show the money | 1 min |
| 8. Next Steps | Clear call to action | 30 sec |
| 9. Questions | Discussion | 5 min |
| 10. Thank You | Close professionally | — |

---

## Slide 1: Title

**Content:**
- Your logo + partner logo
- Title: "Partnership Opportunity: [Your Company] + [Partner Company]"
- Date and presenter

---

## Slide 2: The Problem

**Headline:** The Challenge [Your Target Market] Faces

**Content:**
- What's broken in the market?
- What do customers struggle with?
- Why is now the time to act?

**Example:**
"Companies spend 40% more than necessary on legacy solutions because they don't have access to modern alternatives."

---

## Slide 3: Our Solution

**Headline:** How We Solve It

**Content:**
- Your solution in 1-2 sentences
- Key differentiators (3 max)
- Why it matters to customers

**Example:**
"We provide a unified platform that reduces costs by 30% while improving outcomes. Our customers see ROI in 90 days."

---

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity

**Headline:** The Opportunity We're Attacking

**Content:**
- Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Growth rate
- Why this moment matters

**Example:**
- TAM: $50B
- SAM: $12B (our addressable)
- Growth: 25% annually

---

## Slide 5: The Partnership

**Headline:** How We Win Together

**Content:**
- What you're offering partners
- Partner benefits (3-4 max)
- What makes this unique

**Example:**
- Co-sell opportunities in [vertical]
- [X]% revenue share
- Joint marketing funds
- Dedicated partner support

---

## Slide 6: Success Stories

**Headline:** Partners Who've Succeeded

**Content:**
- 2-3 partner success stories
- Include specific numbers
- Before/after where possible

**Example:**
"[Partner Name] grew revenue 140% in 18 months. They generated $2M in new pipeline and reduced customer acquisition cost by 35%."

---

## Slide 7: Partnership Economics

**Headline:** The Business Case

**Content:**
- Revenue potential
- Investment required
- Timeline to ROI

**Example:**
- Typical partner revenue: $200K/year
- Investment required: $50K (training + marketing)
- Payback period: 6 months

---

## Slide 8: Next Steps

**Headline:** Let's Get Started

**Content:**
- Clear 3-step process
- Timeline
- Contact information

**Example:**
1. Sign partnership agreement (Week 1)
2. Complete partner onboarding (Weeks 2-3)
3. First co-sell deal (Week 6)

---

## Slide 9: Questions

**Content:**
- "What questions do you have?"
- Be prepared for common objections

---

## Slide 10: Thank You

**Content:**
- Thank them for their time
- Reiterate key contact
- Send deck + follow-up within 24 hours

---

## Customization by Partner Type

### For Resellers

- Emphasize margins and territory protection
- Include competitive displacement data
- Show pricing and discount structure

### For System Integrators

- Emphasize implementation services revenue
- Show certification path
- Include enablement investment

### For Technology Partners

- Emphasize integration opportunities
- Show joint solution potential
- Include co-development options

---

## Common Pitfalls

**Pitfall #1: Too Many Slides**
- **What happens:** Lose audience attention
- **Warning sign:** >15 slides
- **Prevention:** 10 slides max

**Pitfall #2: No Numbers**
- **What happens:** Not persuasive
- **Warning sign:** Vague claims
- **Prevention:** Every claim needs proof

**Pitfall #3: No Clear Ask**
- **What happens:** Great meeting, no action
- **Warning sign:** "Let's stay in touch"
- **Prevention:** Always end with next steps
