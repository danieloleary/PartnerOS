1. Check your inbox for an invitation from [noreply@yourcompany.com]

3. Complete your profile (company, role, phone)
4. Accept the Partner Agreement (if not already signed)

## DEAL REGISTRATION

**Where:** Deals → Register New Deal

### How to Register

1. Click **+ New Deal**
2. Fill in prospect details:
   - Company name and website
   - Primary contact (name, title, email)
   - Product / solution interested in
   - Estimated deal value
   - Expected close date
3. Describe your engagement (how you found them, meeting held)
4. Submit — you'll receive a confirmation email within [2] business hours

### Deal Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Submitted | Received; under review |
| Approved | Protected — commission applies |
| Rejected | See reason in notes; appeal if needed |
| Expired | Protection period ended; re-register if still active |
| Closed Won | Commission will be calculated |
| Closed Lost | No commission; no impact on standing |

> **Tip:** Register deals within 5 business days of your first qualifying call.
> Late registrations are not guaranteed protection.

## MDF (MARKETING DEVELOPMENT FUNDS)

**Where:** MDF → My Balance / New Request

### Check Your Balance

Dashboard shows:
- **Allocated:** Funds available this quarter
- **Committed:** Approved but not yet spent
- **Available:** Allocated minus committed

### How to Request MDF

1. MDF → New Request
2. Describe the activity (event, content, campaign)
3. Upload a brief (what, when, expected outcome)
4. Submit for approval (allow [5] business days)
5. After activity, submit proof of execution + invoice for reimbursement

### Eligible Activities

| Activity | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Events / conferences | [Company] logo required on materials |
| Digital advertising | Pre-approved messaging required |
| Content creation | Must feature [Company] solution |
| Partner-hosted webinars | [Company] speaker participation encouraged |
| Sales tools / demos | Technical tools that accelerate pipeline |

### Ineligible Activities

- General overhead or staff costs
- Travel to [Company] headquarters
- Activities already reimbursed elsewhere
- Activities not pre-approved
