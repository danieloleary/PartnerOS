## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
Monthly reports enforce a forcing function: PAMs who fill this out consistently
catch partner drift early and have the data they need for QBRs and board updates.

**Instructions:**
1. Fill out the prior month's data by the [5th] of each month
2. Share with [VP/Director of Partnerships] for review
3. Use the at-risk section to request internal support before QBR
