## Template: Email Sequence for Partner Recruitment

**Original template:** `docs/recruitment/01-email-sequence.md`

**Company:** TechCorp Solutions
**Partner Type:** Managed Service Provider (MSP)
**Industry:** SMB IT Services

---

## Email 1: Initial Outreach

**Subject:** Partnership Opportunity - TechCorp + [Partner Company]

Hi [Partner Name],

I came across [Company] and I'm impressed by what you're building in the [Industry] space.

At TechCorp Solutions, we're looking for strategic partners to help us deliver enterprise-grade solutions to mid-market companies like yours.

I'd love to explore if there might be a natural fit between our organizations.

**Would you be open to a 15-minute call next week?**

Best regards,

Sarah Johnson  
Partner Manager, TechCorp Solutions  
sarah@techcorpsolutions.com | (555) 123-4567

---

## Email 2: Follow-Up (3 days later)

**Subject:** Following Up - TechCorp Partnership

Hi [Partner Name],

I wanted to follow up on my previous email about a potential partnership between TechCorp Solutions and [Company].

We see a strong fit because:
- Similar target market (mid-market companies)
- Complementary service offerings
- Shared commitment to customer success

Would you have 15 minutes this week for a quick call?

Best,  
Sarah

---

## Email 3: Value Proposition

**Subject:** Why TechCorp + [Partner] Could Be Powerful

Hi [Partner Name],

I wanted to share more about what makes TechCorp a strong partner:

**What We Offer Partners:**
- 20% revenue share on all sourced deals
- Co-marketing funds (MDF) for joint campaigns
- Priority lead delivery for your territory
- Dedicated partner success manager
- Weekly enablement calls

**What We're Looking For:**
- Established customer base (50+ active clients)
- Technical certification capability
- Sales team of 3+ dedicated account executives
- Experience with enterprise software sales

Looking forward to exploring this further.

Best,  
Sarah

---

## Email 4: Call to Action

**Subject:** Let's Schedule - Partnership Discussion

Hi [Partner Name],

I'll be in [Partner City] next week and would love to meet in person if possible.

Are you available for a lunch & learn about our partnership program?

If not, I'm happy to do a virtual call at your convenience.

**Book time directly:** https://calendly.com/sarah-techcorp

Best,  
Sarah

---

## Notes on Customization

This example shows:
- **Professional tone** - Not too salesy, focused on value
- **Specific details** - Revenue share %, team sizes
- **Clear CTAs** - Easy ways to respond
- **Follow-up sequence** - 4 emails over 2 weeks

Replace bracketed text [like this] with your specific details.
