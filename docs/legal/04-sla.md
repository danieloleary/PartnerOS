## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
Use this Service Level Agreement to define support commitments between your company and partners.

**Steps:**
1. Define support scope and hours
2. Set response time targets by severity
3. Establish escalation procedures
4. Have legal review
5. Execute with partner

# Service Level Agreement

## PARTIES

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") is entered into as of [Date] and forms part of the Master Service Agreement dated [MSA Date] between:

**[Your Company Name]** ("Company")  
AND  
**[Partner Company Name]** ("Partner")

## 1. SUPPORT SCOPE

### 1.1 Covered Services
This SLA applies to:
- [Product/Service Name]
- [Product/Service Name]
- [API/Integration support]

### 1.2 Support Hours
| Tier | Support Hours |
|------|---------------|
| Bronze | Business hours (9am-5pm PT, M-F) |
| Silver | Business hours + Next business day response |
| Gold | 24/7 support with 4-hour response |

### 1.3 Support Channels
- Email: [support@company.com]
- Portal: [support.company.com]
- Phone: [1-800-XXX-XXXX]
- Partner Portal: [partners.company.com/support]

## 2. RESPONSE TIMES

### 2.1 Severity Definitions
| Severity | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Critical (P1)** | Service completely unavailable | System down, data loss |
| **High (P2)** | Major feature unavailable | Login failure, payment issues |
| **Medium (P3)** | Minor feature impaired | UI bug, performance issue |
| **Low (P4)** | General inquiry | How-to questions |

### 2.2 Response Time Targets
| Severity | Silver Partner | Gold Partner |
|----------|---------------|--------------|
| Critical (P1) | 8 business hours | 4 hours |
| High (P2) | 16 business hours | 8 hours |
| Medium (P3) | 3 business days | 1 business day |
| Low (P4) | 5 business days | 2 business days |

### 2.3 Resolution Targets
| Severity | Target Resolution Time |
|----------|------------------------|
| Critical (P1) | 24 hours |
| High (P2) | 72 hours |
| Medium (P3) | 5 business days |
| Low (P4) | 10 business days |

## 3. ESCALATION PROCEDURES

### 3.1 Escalation Path
```
Level 1: Support Team (Tier 1)
    ↓ No resolution in [X] hours
Level 2: Support Manager (Tier 2)
    ↓ No resolution in [X] hours  
Level 3: Director of Support
    ↓ No resolution in [X] hours
Level 4: VP of Customer Success
```

### 3.2 Escalation Contacts
| Level | Name | Title | Contact |
|-------|------|-------|---------|
| 1 | [Name] | Support Lead | [email/phone] |
| 2 | [Name] | Support Manager | [email/phone] |
| 3 | [Name] | Support Director | [email/phone] | [Name] |
| 4 | VP Customer Success | [email/phone] |

### 3.3 Escalation Triggers
- Critical issue unresolved for [4] hours
- High priority issue unresolved for [24] hours
- Partner requests escalation
- Customer threat to escalate externally

## 4. SERVICE CREDITS

### 4.1 Credit Structure
If Company fails to meet response time commitments, Partner is eligible for:

| Missed Target | Service Credit |
|---------------|---------------|
| Critical response time | 10% of monthly fees |
| High response time | 5% of monthly fees |
| 3+ consecutive misses | 25% of monthly fees |

### 4.2 Limitations
- Maximum credit: [25]% of monthly fees
- Credits applied to next billing cycle
- Exclusions for:
  - Force majeure
  - Partner-caused issues
  - Scheduled maintenance (with 48hr notice)

## 5. MAINTENANCE WINDOWS

### 5.1 Scheduled Maintenance
- Maintenance window: [Sunday 2am-6am PT]
- Notice period: 48 hours
- Impact: [Describe typical impact]

### 5.2 Emergency Maintenance
- May be required for critical security patches
- Partner notification within [4] hours
- Best effort to minimize downtime

## 6. REPORTING

### 6.1 Monthly Reports
Company shall provide monthly reports including:
- Total tickets opened/closed
- Average response time by severity
- Resolution time metrics
- Open issues

### 6.2 Quarterly Business Review
SLA performance reviewed during QBR including:
- Trend analysis
- Improvement plans
- Partner feedback

## 7. TERM

This SLA shall be effective for [1] year from the Effective Date and shall automatically renew unless either party provides [30] days written notice.
