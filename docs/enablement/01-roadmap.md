## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
Design a structured, measurable enablement journey that transforms new partners into high-performing, certified experts—especially for complex enterprise solutions with hardware and software components.

**Steps:**
1. Assess partner's current skill level using the baseline assessment
2. Customize learning paths based on partner type and goals
3. Assign owners and track progress in your LMS/PRM
4. Conduct monthly enablement check-ins
5. Celebrate certifications and milestones

**Pro Tips:**
- Technical depth is the #1 predictor of partner success in enterprise sales
- Front-load enablement—the first 90 days determine long-term productivity
- Make certifications a prerequisite for deal support and higher margins
