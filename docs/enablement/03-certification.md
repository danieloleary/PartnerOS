## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
Use this template to design and implement a comprehensive partner certification program that validates competencies, drives enablement adoption, and differentiates high-performing partners.

**Steps:**
1. Define certification tracks aligned to partner roles and your product portfolio.
2. Establish prerequisites, learning paths, and assessment criteria for each certification.
3. Create the exam/assessment content and passing thresholds.
4. Set up credential management and badge distribution.
5. Link certifications to program tier requirements and benefits.

# Partner Certification Program Framework

## 1. Program Overview

### Purpose & Strategic Value
A well-designed certification program serves multiple strategic objectives:

- **Quality Assurance:** Ensures partners have validated skills to represent your brand and deliver customer value.
- **Differentiation:** Helps certified partners stand out in the market and win more business.
- **Enablement Adoption:** Drives completion of training content by making certification a requirement or incentive.
- **Customer Confidence:** Provides customers assurance that they're working with qualified partners.
- **Partner Retention:** Creates investment and stickiness in the partnership.

### Program Principles
- **Accessible:** Training and certification should be available on-demand with clear paths.
- **Relevant:** Content must reflect real-world skills needed to succeed with your solutions.
- **Valuable:** Certifications should provide tangible benefits (badges, program tier advancement, co-marketing eligibility).
- **Current:** Content must be updated regularly to reflect product and market changes.

## 2. Certification Tracks

### Track 1: Sales Certification
**Target Audience:** Partner sales representatives and account executives

**Levels:**
| Level | Name | Requirements | Validity |
|-------|------|--------------|----------|
| Foundation | Certified Sales Associate | Complete Sales 101 training, pass online exam (70%+) | 1 year |
| Advanced | Certified Sales Professional | Foundation + Advanced Sales training, mock pitch review | 1 year |
| Expert | Certified Sales Expert | Professional + 5 closed deals, case study submission | 2 years |

**Learning Path:**
1. Product overview and value proposition
2. Target customer profile and pain points
3. Competitive positioning and objection handling
4. Deal registration and co-selling process
5. Pricing and packaging fundamentals

**Assessment Format:**
- Online multiple-choice exam (40 questions, 60 minutes)
- Practical: Recorded pitch or live presentation to certification team

---

### Track 2: Technical Certification
**Target Audience:** Solution architects, implementation consultants, technical pre-sales

**Levels:**
| Level | Name | Requirements | Validity |
|-------|------|--------------|----------|
| Foundation | Certified Technical Associate | Complete Technical 101 training, pass online exam (75%+) | 1 year |
| Advanced | Certified Technical Professional | Foundation + hands-on lab completion, architecture review | 1 year |
| Expert | Certified Technical Expert | Professional + 3 successful implementations, advanced lab | 2 years |

**Learning Path:**
1. Architecture and infrastructure fundamentals
2. Installation, configuration, and deployment
3. Integration patterns and API usage
4. Security, compliance, and best practices
5. Troubleshooting and support procedures

**Assessment Format:**
- Online multiple-choice exam (50 questions, 90 minutes)
- Practical: Hands-on lab environment assessment
- Expert: Architecture design review with certification team

---

### Track 3: Delivery/Implementation Certification
**Target Audience:** Professional services consultants, implementation specialists

**Levels:**
| Level | Name | Requirements | Validity |
|-------|------|--------------|----------|
| Foundation | Certified Implementation Associate | Technical Foundation + Delivery methodology training | 1 year |
| Advanced | Certified Implementation Professional | Associate + 2 supervised implementations | 1 year |
| Expert | Certified Implementation Expert | Professional + 5 independent implementations, peer review | 2 years |

**Learning Path:**
1. Implementation methodology and project phases
2. Requirements gathering and solution design
3. Data migration and integration best practices
4. Testing, QA, and go-live procedures
5. Customer success handoff and documentation

**Assessment Format:**
- Online exam covering methodology and best practices
- Practical: Implementation plan review
- Expert: Full project documentation and customer reference

| Partner Tier | Sales Certs Required | Technical Certs Required | Benefits Unlocked |
|--------------|---------------------|-------------------------|-------------------|

| Silver | 1 Sales Associate | 1 Technical Associate | Deal registration, basic support |
| Gold | 2 Sales Professional | 2 Technical Professional | Co-marketing, MDF access, priority support |

## 4. Exam & Assessment Standards

### Exam Development
- **Question Bank:** Maintain 3x the number of questions needed per exam for randomization.
- **Review Cycle:** Update questions quarterly to reflect product changes.
- **Subject Matter Experts:** Involve product, sales, and customer success teams in question development.
- **Beta Testing:** Test new exams with internal team before partner launch.

### Scoring & Passing
| Certification Level | Passing Score | Retake Policy | Waiting Period |
|--------------------|---------------|---------------|----------------|
| Foundation | 70% | 3 attempts included | 7 days between attempts |
| Professional | 75% | 2 attempts included | 14 days between attempts |
| Expert | 80% | 1 attempt included | 30 days between attempts |

### Proctoring Options
- **Self-Proctored:** Foundation level exams with webcam monitoring
- **Live Proctored:** Professional and Expert level exams with remote proctor
- **In-Person:** Available at partner events or regional offices

## 5. Credential Management

### Digital Badges
- Issue digital badges via Credly, Accredible, or similar platform
- Badges should be shareable on LinkedIn, email signatures, and websites
- Include verification link for customer validation

### Badge Design Standards
```
[Badge Elements]
- Certification name and level
- Your company logo
- Issue date and expiration
- Unique verification ID
- QR code for instant verification
```

### Certification Directory
- Maintain a public directory of certified partners on your website
- Allow customers to search by certification type, location, and specialty
- Include partner contact information and certification details

## 6. Recertification & Maintenance

### Annual Requirements
| Certification Level | Recertification Requirement |
|--------------------|----------------------------|
| Foundation | Retake exam OR complete annual update training |
| Professional | Complete annual update training + brief assessment |
| Expert | Annual update training + demonstrate continued activity |

### Grace Period
- 30-day grace period after expiration for recertification
- After grace period, must retake full certification path

### Lapsed Certification
- Notify partner 60, 30, and 7 days before expiration
- Provide streamlined recertification path for recently lapsed certifications (within 90 days)
- After 90 days, partner must complete full certification path

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|-----------------|
| Certification Program Manager | Overall program strategy, content standards, reporting |

| Partner Manager | Encourage certification, track partner progress |

### Tracking & Reporting
- Track certification status in PRM/CRM
- Generate monthly reports on certification completion rates
- Identify partners at risk of lapsing
- Measure correlation between certification and partner performance
