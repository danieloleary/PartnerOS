## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
Deal registration gives partners credit — and deal protection — for opportunities
they source or develop. A clear policy reduces conflict, increases partner trust,
and ensures you capture accurate pipeline attribution.

**Steps:**
1. Define eligibility criteria (what qualifies for registration)
2. Set protection periods by tier
3. Configure your CRM/PRM to enforce the rules
4. Communicate to all partners during onboarding

# Deal Registration Policy

**Effective Date:** [Date]
**Applies to:** All authorized partners of [Company Name]
**Administered by:** [Partner Operations]

---

## PURPOSE

This policy establishes rules for partners to register sales opportunities
with [Company], receive deal protection during the sales cycle, and earn
appropriate credit upon close.

---

## ELIGIBILITY — WHAT QUALIFIES FOR REGISTRATION

A deal is eligible for registration if **ALL** of the following are true:

- [ ] The prospect is not an existing [Company] customer
- [ ] The prospect is not already registered to another partner
- [ ] The prospect is not an active [Company] direct opportunity (in CRM < [30] days)
- [ ] The expected deal value exceeds $[minimum deal size]
- [ ] Expected close date is within [180] days
- [ ] Partner has had a qualifying interaction with the prospect (meeting, call, demo)

### Qualifying Interactions

A "qualifying interaction" is documented evidence of partner engagement:

| Interaction | Qualifies? |
|-------------|-----------|
| Discovery call with economic buyer | ✅ Yes |
| Demo or proof of concept | ✅ Yes |
| Proposal or business case presented | ✅ Yes |
| Email chain with prospect only | ❌ No |
| Cold prospect list submission | ❌ No |
| LinkedIn connection only | ❌ No |

---

## REGISTRATION PROCESS

### Step 1 — Submit Registration

Partners submit via [Partner Portal / CRM link] within **[5 business days]**
of qualifying interaction.

Required fields:
- Company name and primary contact
- Estimated deal value
- Expected close date
- Product / solution of interest
- Brief description of opportunity and partner involvement

### Step 2 — Review and Approval

| Outcome | Timeframe | Meaning |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Approved | Within [2] business days | Deal protected; partner credited |
| Pending | [2–5] business days | Under review; do not proceed |
| Rejected | Within [2] business days | Reason provided; appeal available |

### Step 3 — Protection Begins

Protection starts on **approval date**, not submission date.

---

## DEAL PROTECTION PERIODS

| Partner Tier | Protection Period | Extension Available? |
|-------------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Registered | [30] days | No |
| Silver | [60] days | +30 days (request required) |
| Gold | [90] days | +60 days (automatic if active) |
| Strategic | [120] days | +90 days (with business justification) |

### Extension Requests

Extensions require:
- Documented evidence of continued active engagement
- Updated close date from prospect
- Submitted to [partner-ops@company.com] at least [5] business days before expiration

---

## COMMISSION / CREDIT ON REGISTERED DEALS

| Scenario | Credit |
|----------|--------|
| Partner sources AND closes the deal | Full partner-sourced commission |
| Partner sources, [Company] assists in close | Partner-sourced commission |
| Partner sources, [Company] closes independently | [50%] of partner-sourced commission |
| Registration expires, [Company] closes later | No credit (re-registration required) |

---

## CHANNEL CONFLICT RESOLUTION

### When Two Partners Register the Same Prospect

1. **First registration wins** — approval timestamp is determinative
2. Second partner notified within [2] business days
3. If registered within [24 hours] of each other:
   - Both parties contacted
   - [Company] determines primary partner based on evidence of engagement
   - Decision is final and communicated within [5] business days

### When [Company] Direct and Partner Conflict

- If [Company] has active opportunity (in CRM > [30] days): **direct team wins**
- If [Company] opportunity < [30] days old at time of partner registration: **co-sell required**
- If [Company] has no prior activity: **partner wins**

---

## PROHIBITED PRACTICES

Partners may NOT:

- Register competitors, inactive prospects, or speculative targets without engagement
- Register prospects they have not directly engaged
- Submit bulk registrations without qualifying interactions
- Share deal registration approvals with unaffiliated third parties

Violations may result in registration cancellation, commission forfeiture,
or partner agreement termination.

---

## APPEALS PROCESS

1. Submit written appeal to [partner-ops@company.com] within [10 business days]
2. Include supporting evidence (emails, meeting notes, call logs)
3. Decision within [15 business days]
4. Appeals reviewed by Partner Operations Director; decision is final
