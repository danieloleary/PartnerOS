## How to Use This Template

**Purpose:**
Operationalize a world-class onboarding experience that gets enterprise partners to first revenue fastâ€”especially for complex, multi-product solutions with hardware and software components.

**Steps:**
1. Customize phases based on partner type and complexity
2. Assign owners and due dates in your PRM/project tool
3. Conduct weekly check-ins during first 30 days, bi-weekly thereafter
4. Track health score and intervene early if off-track
5. Celebrate milestones and recognize early wins

**Pro Tips:**
- Front-load relationship buildingâ€”the first 30 days set the tone
- For enterprise partners, involve exec sponsors early
- Technical enablement is the #1 predictor of partner success

## Partner Health Score

Calculate weekly during onboarding:

| Factor | Weight | Score (1-5) | Weighted |
|--------|--------|-------------|----------|
| Portal engagement | 15% | [X] | [X] |
| Training progress | 20% | [X] | [X] |
| Meeting attendance | 10% | [X] | [X] |
| Pipeline activity | 25% | [X] | [X] |
| Responsiveness | 15% | [X] | [X] |
| Exec engagement | 15% | [X] | [X] |
| **Total** | **100%** | | **[X]** |

**Score Interpretation:**
- **4.0-5.0 (Green):** On track, continue current cadence
- **3.0-3.9 (Yellow):** At risk, increase engagement
- **2.0-2.9 (Orange):** Struggling, escalate to exec sponsor
- **1.0-1.9 (Red):** Critical, intervention required
