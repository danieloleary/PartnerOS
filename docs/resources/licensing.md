# PartnerOS Licensing & Pricing

*Last Updated: February 2026*

---

## Overview

PartnerOS is available in multiple tiers to fit different needs:

- **Open Source** - Free for self-service use
- **Implementation** - Paid setup and customization
- **Enterprise** - Full-service solution

---

## Option 1: Self-Service (Free)

**What's Included:**
- 44+ partner program templates
- AI Partner Agent (self-hosted)
- Documentation and guides
- Community support via GitHub

**Requirements:**
- Self-host (GitHub clone or download)
- Run locally or on your own servers
- Set up your own AI (Ollama, Anthropic, OpenAI)

**Cost:** FREE

---

## Option 2: Implementation

**What's Included:**
- Everything in Self-Service
- 2 hours of implementation consulting
- Custom template configuration
- Branded version (your logo/colors)
- 30-day email support

**Best For:** Companies who want help getting started

**Cost:** $2,500 one-time

## Option 3: Enterprise

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I upgrade later?
**A:** Yes! You can upgrade from Self-Service to Implementation or Enterprise at any time. We'll credit what you've already paid.

### Q: Do you offer refunds?
**A:** Yes, within 30 days for Implementation and Enterprise packages if you're not satisfied.

### Q: Can I use PartnerOS for multiple companies?
**A:** Implementation and Enterprise licenses include use for one company. Additional companies require additional licenses. Contact us for multi-company pricing.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** Credit card, wire transfer, ACH. Annual contracts can be invoiced.

### Q: Is there a trial period?
**A:** Self-Service is free (try it!). For paid tiers, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee.
