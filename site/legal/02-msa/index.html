    How to Use This Template¶

    Purpose: Use this Master Service Agreement template to establish the legal and commercial framework for a formal partnership.

    Steps: 1. Customize all bracketed sections 2. Define partnership terms and conditions 3. Establish payment and commission structures 4. Have legal review 5. Execute with partner

    Master Service Agreement¶

    PARTIES¶

    This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] by and between:

    [Your Company Name] ("Company") [Address] [City, State, ZIP]

    AND

    [Partner Company Name] ("Partner") [Address] [City, State, ZIP]

    1. DEFINITIONS¶

    "Services" means the [products/services] provided by Partner to End Customers under this Agreement.

    "End Customer" means any third party that purchases Services from Partner.

    "Confidential Information" has the meaning set forth in the Mutual NDA executed between the parties.

    "Effective Date" means the date first written above.

    2. PARTNERSHIP STRUCTURE¶

    2.1 Partner Classification¶

    Partner is designated as a [Reseller / System Integrator / Strategic Partner] under the [Bronze / Silver / Gold] tier of Company's Partner Program.

    2.2 Territory¶

    Partner is authorized to promote and sell Services in the following territory: [Global / Specific Countries / Specific Regions]

    2.3 Exclusivity¶

    [ ] Non-Exclusive: Partner may represent other companies selling competing products. [ ] Exclusive: Partner is the only authorized partner for [specific products/territory].

    3. OBLIGATIONS OF PARTNER¶

    Partner agrees to:

    3.1 Sales and Marketing¶

    • Actively promote and sell Services to End Customers
    • Conduct marketing activities in accordance with Company's guidelines
    • Participate in quarterly business reviews
    • Provide timely forecasts and pipeline reports

    3.2 Technical Capabilities¶

    • Maintain qualified technical staff to sell and implement Services
    • Complete required certifications within [90] days of agreement execution
    • Provide first-level support to End Customers

    3.3 Compliance¶

    • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
    • Conduct business in a professional manner consistent with Company's brand
    • Maintain appropriate insurance coverage

    4. OBLIGATIONS OF COMPANY¶

    Company agrees to:

    4.1 Support¶

    • Provide technical support as outlined in Exhibit A
    • Offer training and certification programs
    • Assign a Partner Account Manager

    4.2 Resources¶

    • Make available sales and technical materials
    • Provide pre-sales support for opportunities exceeding [threshold]
    • Include Partner in co-marketing activities

    4.3 Deal Registration¶

    • Protect registered opportunities for [90] days
    • Not sell directly to registered End Customers during protection period

    5. PAYMENT TERMS¶

    5.1 Pricing¶

    Company shall sell Services to Partner at the prices set forth in Exhibit B (Partner Price List).

    5.2 Discount Structure¶

    Tier Discount from List Price
    Bronze [X]%
    Silver [X]%
    Gold [X]%

    5.3 Payment Terms¶

    • Net [30 / 45 / 60] days from invoice date
    • Late payments subject to [X]% per month interest

    5.4 Revenue Share¶

    For partner-sourced deals: Partner receives [X]% of net revenue.

    6. WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS¶

    6.1 Mutual Representations¶

    Each party represents and warrants that: - It is duly organized and in good standing - It has full power and authority to enter into this Agreement - This Agreement constitutes a legal, valid, and binding obligation

    6.2 Company Warranties¶

    Company warrants that: - Services will perform substantially in accordance with documentation - Company has proper rights to license all software included in Services

    6.3 Disclaimer¶

    EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH HEREIN, SERVICES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS."

    7. INDEMNIFICATION¶

    7.1 Company Indemnification¶

    Company shall indemnify Partner against claims that Services infringe third-party intellectual property rights.

    7.2 Partner Indemnification¶

    Partner shall indemnify Company against claims arising from: - Partner's negligence or misconduct - Misrepresentation of Services to End Customers - Violation of applicable laws

    8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY¶

    EXCEPT FOR INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS, NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES. TOTAL LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED [Amount].

    9. TERM AND TERMINATION¶

    9.1 Initial Term¶

    This Agreement shall be effective for [1 / 2 / 3] year(s) from the Effective Date.

    9.2 Renewal¶

    This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive [1-year] periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least [60] days prior to expiration.

    9.3 Termination for Cause¶

    Either party may terminate this Agreement upon [30] days written notice if the other party: - Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within the notice period - Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

    9.4 Effect of Termination¶

    • Partner shall immediately cease representing itself as a Partner
    • Partner shall complete all pending orders within [30] days
    • Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination

    10. GENERAL PROVISIONS¶

    10.1 Governing Law¶

    This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [State/Country].

    10.2 Dispute Resolution¶

    [Mediation / Arbitration] in [City, State/Country].

    10.3 Assignment¶

    Neither party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent, except to a successor in connection with a merger or sale of substantially all assets.

    10.4 Entire Agreement¶

    This Agreement, including all Exhibits, constitutes the entire agreement and supersedes all prior agreements.

    SIGNATURES¶

    COMPANY:

    Signature: _______________________ Name: _______________________ Title: _______________________ Date: _______________________

    PARTNER:

    Signature: _______________________ Name: _______________________ Title: _______________________ Date: _______________________

    EXHIBITS¶

    • Exhibit A: Support Terms and Service Level Agreement
    • Exhibit B: Partner Price List and Discount Schedule
    • Exhibit C: Certification Requirements
    • Exhibit D: Marketing Guidelines

    Checklist¶

    • Customize all bracketed sections
    • Define tier-specific terms
    • Set pricing and discount structure
    • Establish territory and exclusivity terms
    • Have legal counsel review
    • Execute with signatures
    • Create Exhibits A-D
